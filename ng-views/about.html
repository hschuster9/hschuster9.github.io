My Story




Growing up during a time when technology quickly changed our lives, I have always been curious about the design and aesthetics of new products. I found myself constantly researching the latest advancements to learn as much as I could about each one.

After realizing that the tech industry is more than an interest, I took the leap into web development. From rendering my first, “Hello World”, to building multiple full-stack applications, I have been amazed by the web's capabilities. I am very passionate about learning exactly what goes into creating well-designed and responsive websites. With a background in Business, I also understand the importance of collaboration in order to build large scale applications.

Every day is a new challenge and I am eager to continue exploring the endless possibilites in web development.

Outside of coding, I enjoy cooking for others, going to spinning classes, and finding ways to entertain my energetic dog in a busy city.
